title: Connect a device to the network
lang: en
---

{/* t:translated-from fr */}

To access the cloud services from a smartphone, a tablet or a personal computer.

## Within a zone

A device connected to a gateway's network automatically becomes part of the
network and accesses services after a few settings.

## From outside

Outside a zone, you need the Tailscale VPN client, validated by the administrator
(see the
[administrator guide](/en/doc/admin-guide/first-network-installation/#connecting-from-outside)).
