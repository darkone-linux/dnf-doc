## Within a zone

A device connected to a gateway's network automatically becomes part of the
network and accesses services after a few settings.

{/* t:p e12f67912b2fedc41cc79e8819a45f24f12b2b42bfba25977ac390ea88a74412 */}
## From outside

Outside a zone, you need the Tailscale VPN client, approved by the
administrator (see the
[admin guide](/en/doc/admin-guide/install/hcs/#connect-from-outside)).
