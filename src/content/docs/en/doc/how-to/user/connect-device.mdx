Access your photos, files and services from a smartphone, tablet or personal
computer. At home, it's automatic; elsewhere, a secure little app bridges the
gap.

{/* t:p 59b3e1130b289a1414c557e33b2652e64bd4b5a39c345bc036b00ef6f255fb92 */}
## Inside a zone

At home (or anywhere with a gateway), there's virtually nothing to do:

<Steps>

1. **Connect the device** to the WiFi or the zone's internal network.
2. **You're done.** Addressing, Internet access (filtered, ad-free) and services
   are configured automatically.
3. **Open the zone's homepage** in your browser: all services are listed there.

</Steps>

{/* t:p 240e99f33bc41575acd8c0b880918ff04b938ee7a5da4d1e4c8d746ce3015c48 */}
## A single identity

A **single username** unlocks all services (SSO). Log in once and find your
access everywhere.

- **Forgot your password?** Ask your administrator to reset it.
- **Mobile apps** (photos, files, email): enter the service address provided by
  the administrator, then your usual username.

## From outside

Outside a zone, a secure application (Tailscale VPN client) connects your
device to the network, as if you were at home.

:::note[Often unnecessary]
Most services published online are already accessible **without a VPN**,
directly from your browser. This step is only needed for services internal to
a zone.
:::

<Steps>

1. **Install Tailscale** on the device (iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, Linux).
2. **Log in** to the server provided by your administrator.
3. **Wait for approval** from the administrator: your device then joins the
   network and its services.

</Steps>

:::tip[Admin side]
Approving a remote device is described in the
[administrator's guide](/en/doc/admin-guide/install/hcs/#connect-from-outside).
:::
