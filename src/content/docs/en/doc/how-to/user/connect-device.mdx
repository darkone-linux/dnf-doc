## Within a zone

A device connected to a gateway's network automatically becomes part of the
network and accesses services after a few settings.

{/* t:p be040d1efd8c09f45ed2729e39214e5f678143719d6caf25bb95f29b738c5457 */}
## From outside

Outside a zone, you need the Tailscale VPN client, validated by the administrator
(see the
[administrator guide](/en/doc/admin-guide/install/first-network-installation/#connecting-from-outside)).
