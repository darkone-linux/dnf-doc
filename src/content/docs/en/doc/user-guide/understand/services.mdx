## Online Services

All cloud services are open-source and have their own online documentation. But here are some useful introductions.

### Vaultwarden

**Vaultwarden** is my digital vault: passwords, one-time codes (2FA), bank cards, and confidential notes. Everything is encrypted, automatically backed up, and by default I'm the only one with access.

Why this instead of LastPass or 1Password? It is **compatible with Bitwarden apps**, but my data stays at home rather than on an editor's server.

:::tip[Useful Features]
- Share credentials with others using "collections".
- Organize passwords and keys into folders.
- Send confidential information using "sends".
:::

:::note[Bitwarden: the Client App]
Bitwarden can be installed as a **browser plugin** or as a **mobile app**.
It makes life easier with automatic form filling of my usernames and passwords, and many other features.
Bitwarden can do everything Vaultwarden (the server that holds the data) can do.
:::

:::caution[Important: Bitwarden Configuration]
By default, Bitwarden is set to "bitwarden.com".
You must select "self-hosted" then enter the Vaultwarden URL to use Bitwarden with the cloud.

⚠️ I MUST NOT ENTER MY MASTER PASSWORD BEFORE DOING THIS.
:::

📖 Documentation: [Vaultwarden](https://github.com/dani-garcia/vaultwarden) and [Bitwarden help](https://bitwarden.com/help/).

### Kanidm (idm)

**Kanidm** is my digital identity guardian: it verifies who I am and opens the doors to all services with **a single login** (SSO). No more remembering one password per service.

Modern and built for security, it goes further than traditional directories:

- Login with **passkeys** and security keys (WebAuthn), passwordless.
- Standard **SSO** (OAuth2 / OpenID Connect) supported by all services.
- Fine-grained **groups and permissions** management.

Daily use is detailed in [Manage my account](/en/doc/user-guide/usage/account/).

📖 Documentation: [Kanidm](https://kanidm.com/).

### Immich (photos)

**Immich** manages my photos and videos with the comfort of Google Photos, but **at home**: no paid quotas, no commercial analysis of my memories.

Highlights:

- **Automatic backup** of smartphone photos (Android / iOS).
- **AI search** and face, place, and object recognition, locally.
- Shared albums, memories, map… a true family photo library.

To sync my smartphone:

1. Install the **Immich** app on the smartphone.
2. Configure backup by entering the Immich URL.

📖 Documentation: [Immich](https://immich.app/).

### Nextcloud (files & data)

**Nextcloud** is my personal cloud, replacing Google Drive or Dropbox: files, contacts, and calendars synced across all my devices, shareable with a link.

Highlights:

- **Synced files** (computer, mobile) and link sharing.
- **Contacts and calendars** (CardDAV / CalDAV) for phone and email.
- **Online document editing**, tasks, forms, and many apps.

📖 Documentation: [Nextcloud](https://nextcloud.com/).

### Matrix (messaging & video)

**Matrix** is my messaging platform, like WhatsApp or Telegram, but **encrypted, open, and self-hosted**. My app is [Element](https://element.io/).

Highlights:

- Messages, files, and **audio / video calls** end-to-end encrypted.
- **Bridges** to WhatsApp, Messenger, Telegram, Signal, Discord… (everything in one app).
- **Federated** network: communicate with other Matrix servers, or stay in a closed environment.

Connection, apps, and bridges are detailed in [Matrix Messaging](/en/doc/user-guide/usage/matrix/).

📖 Documentation: [Matrix](https://matrix.org/).

### Jellyfin (movies & music)

**Jellyfin** is my home media library, like Netflix: movies, TV series, music, and photos, on the TV, computer, or phone.

Highlights:

- **100% free and open source**, no subscription or paid features (unlike Plex).
- Apps for TV, mobile, browser, and media boxes.
- Covers, subtitles, and metadata fetched automatically.

📖 Documentation: [Jellyfin](https://jellyfin.org/).

### Open WebUI (AI)

**Open WebUI** is my generative AI assistant, like ChatGPT, but **conversations never leave home**. It runs on [Ollama](https://ollama.com/) with locally executed models.

Highlights:

- **No data sent** to a third-party service, histories stored locally.
- Multiple **models** to choose from depending on the need.
- Chat with my documents, web search, and custom prompts.

📖 Documentation: [Open WebUI](https://openwebui.com/).

**Outline** is my notes and documentation space, personal or shared, like Notion but free and sovereign.

- Fast **markdown** editor with powerful search.
- Organized into **collections** and nested documents.
- Sharing, collaborative writing, and checklists.

📖 Documentation: [Outline](https://www.getoutline.com/).

**Mealie** organizes all my cooking recipes and helps plan meals.

Highlights:

- **Import a recipe** from a simple URL.

- Categories, tags, and search.

### Searxng (search)

**SearXNG** is my **ad-free, tracker-free** search engine. It queries dozens of other engines (Google, Bing…) and aggregates results without revealing my identity.

Highlights:

- **Metasearch engine**: cross-references many sources in a single search.
- No **ads**, no advertising profile built about me.
- Image, video, map, news search…

📖 Documentation: [SearXNG](https://docs.searxng.org/).

### AdguardHome (ad-blocking firewall)

**AdGuard Home** filters ads, trackers, and malicious sites for **the whole network**, directly at the gateway. Result: faster and safer browsing on all devices, without installing anything on each one.

Highlights:

- **Network-level** (DNS) blocking of ads and trackers, for all devices.
- Protection against **malicious** domains and tracking.
- **Parental control** and per-device filtering.

📖 Documentation: [AdGuard Home](https://github.com/AdguardTeam/AdGuardHome).

### Forgejo (git forge)

**Forgejo** is my software forge, a personal and sovereign GitHub (free fork of [Gitea](https://about.gitea.com/)). Beyond code, Git is also an excellent way to organize, version, and back up documents.

Highlights:

- Git repositories, **issue** tracking, and **pull requests**.
- **100% free**, with no corporate dependency.
- Continuous integration, wiki, and releases.

📖 Documentation: [Forgejo](https://forgejo.org/).
