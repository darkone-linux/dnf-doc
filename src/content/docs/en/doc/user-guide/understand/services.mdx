## Online services

All cloud services are open-source and have their own online documentation. But here are a few useful introductions.

### Vaultwarden

**Vaultwarden** is my digital vault: passwords, one-time codes (2FA), bank cards, and confidential notes. Everything is encrypted, automatically backed up, and by default only I have access.

Why choose it over LastPass or 1Password? It is **compatible with Bitwarden apps**, but my data stays at home rather than on a vendor's server.

:::caution[Important: Bitwarden configuration]
By default, the Bitwarden client (see below) is set to "bitwarden.com".
You must select "self-hosted" then enter the Vaultwarden URL to use Bitwarden with the cloud.
:::

:::tip[Useful features]
- Share access with others using "collections".
- Organize passwords and keys into folders.
- Send confidential information using "sends".
:::

:::note[Bitwarden: the client application]
Bitwarden can be installed as a **browser plugin** or as a **mobile app**.
It simplifies my life with automatic filling of my credentials and passwords, plus many other features.
Bitwarden can do everything Vaultwarden (the server, which holds the data) can do.
:::

📖 Documentation: [Vaultwarden](https://github.com/dani-garcia/vaultwarden) and [Bitwarden help](https://bitwarden.com/help/).

### Kanidm (idm)

**Kanidm** is the guardian of my digital identity: it verifies who I am and opens the doors to all services with **a single login** (SSO). No need to remember a password for each service.

Modern and built for security, it goes further than traditional directories:

- Login with **passkeys** and security keys (WebAuthn), without a password.
- **SSO** standard (OAuth2 / OpenID Connect) recognized by all services.
- Fine-grained **group and permission** management.

Day-to-day use is detailed in [Managing my account](/en/doc/user-guide/usage/account/).

📖 Documentation: [Kanidm](https://kanidm.com/).

### Immich (photos)

**Immich** manages my photos and videos with the convenience of Google Photos, but **at home**: no paid quota, no commercial analysis of my memories.

Highlights:

- **Automatic backup** of smartphone photos (Android / iOS).
- **AI-powered search** and recognition of faces, places and objects, locally.
- Shared albums, memories, map… a real family photo library.

To sync my smartphone:

1. Install the **Immich** app on the smartphone.
2. Set up the backup by providing the Immich URL.

📖 Documentation: [Immich](https://immich.app/).

### Nextcloud (files & data)

**Nextcloud** is my personal cloud, replacing Google Drive or Dropbox: files, contacts and calendars synced across all my devices, and shareable with a link.

Highlights:

- **Files** synced (computer, mobile) and link sharing.
- **Contacts and calendars** (CardDAV / CalDAV) for phone and email.
- **Online document editing**, tasks, forms and many applications.

📖 Documentation: [Nextcloud](https://nextcloud.com/).

### Matrix (messages & video)

**Matrix** is my messaging platform, like WhatsApp or Telegram, but **encrypted, open and hosted at home**. My app is [Element](https://element.io/).

Highlights:

- Messages, files and **audio / video calls** with end-to-end encryption.
- **Bridges** to WhatsApp, Messenger, Telegram, Signal, Discord… (everything in one app).
- **Federated** network: chat with other Matrix servers, or stay within your own bubble.

Connection, apps and bridges are detailed in [Matrix messaging](/en/doc/user-guide/usage/matrix/).

📖 Documentation: [Matrix](https://matrix.org/).

### Jellyfin (movies & music)

**Jellyfin** is my home media library, like Netflix: movies, series, music and photos, on TV, computer or phone.

Highlights:

- **100% free and open-source**, no subscription or paid features (unlike Plex).
- Apps for TV, mobile, browser and media streaming devices.
- Posters, subtitles and metadata fetched automatically.

📖 Documentation: [Jellyfin](https://jellyfin.org/).

### Open WebUI (AI)

**Open WebUI** is my generative AI assistant, like ChatGPT, but **conversations never leave home**. It runs on [Ollama](https://ollama.com/) with locally-executed models.

Highlights:

- **No data sent** to a third-party service, history stored locally.
- Multiple **models** to choose from depending on the need.
- Chat with my documents, web search and custom prompts.

📖 Documentation: [Open WebUI](https://openwebui.com/).

### Outline (notes)

**Outline** is my notes and documentation space, personal or shared, like Notion but free and sovereign.

Highlights:

- Fast **markdown** editor with powerful search.
- Organization into **collections** and nested documents.
- Sharing, collaborative writing and checklists.

📖 Documentation: [Outline](https://www.getoutline.com/).

**Mealie** stores all my cooking recipes and helps me organize meals.

Highlights:

- **Import a recipe** from a simple URL.
- **Meal planner** and **shopping lists** generated automatically.
- Categories, tags and search.

### Searxng (search)

**SearXNG** is my **ad-free, tracker-free** search engine. It queries dozens of other engines (Google, Bing…) and aggregates results without revealing my identity.

Highlights:

- **Metasearch engine**: cross-references many sources in a single search.
- No **ads**, no advertising profile built behind my back.
- Search for images, videos, maps, news…

📖 Documentation: [SearXNG](https://docs.searxng.org/).

### AdguardHome (ad-blocking firewall)

**AdGuard Home** filters ads, trackers and malicious websites for **the whole network**, directly at the gateway level. Result&nbsp;: faster and safer browsing on all devices, without installing anything on each one.

Key features&nbsp;:

- **Network**-wide (DNS) ad and tracker blocking, for all devices.
- Protection against **malicious** domains and tracking.
- **Parental controls** and per-device filtering.

📖 Docs&nbsp;: [AdGuard Home](https://github.com/AdguardTeam/AdGuardHome).

### Forgejo (git forge)

**Forgejo** is my software forge, a personal and sovereign GitHub (free fork of [Gitea](https://about.gitea.com/)). Beyond code, Git is also an excellent way to organize, version and back up your documents.

Key features&nbsp;:

- Git repositories, **issue** tracking and **pull requests**.
- **100% free**, without dependency on any company.
- Continuous integration, wiki and releases.

📖 Docs&nbsp;: [Forgejo](https://forgejo.org/).
