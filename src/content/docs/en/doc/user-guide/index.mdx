## My sovereign and secure cloud

In short:

- A practical and secure **digital space** for my data.
- **Useful services**: email, video calls, documents, media, backups, games…
- **Pre-installed Linux systems** (optional) that are very easy to use.

:::tip[Ideal for youth & beginners]
A protected space for guided learning of computers and networks&nbsp;!
:::

---

{/* t:p b4f23bfd32a4b99d1e2d393ef4dcb6079b4481de9c7fc2346fc25045aa576bbc */}
### Benefits

* 🔐 **Maximum security**: our data stays controlled and travels encrypted.
* 🚫 **No data leaks** to advertisers, AI, insurance companies, tax authorities...
* 🔁 **Automated 3-2-1 backups**, encrypted, distributed, compressed.
* 🕐 **Top-notch apps**, stable, useful and simple, to save time.
* 🔑 **A single password** to remember (SSO + vault).
* 👶 **User-friendly** for young and old.

:::note[My digital heritage]
Photos, videos, stories, contacts, conversations, passwords... more and more memories and data tied to my heritage are now digital.
Using a safe, sovereign and controlled place for this data, which shapes my identity, is essential. That is the very purpose of this cloud.
:::

:::tip[Should I leave the GAFAM ?]
No. These companies offer useful services. Thousands of engineers work there, making the simplicity and efficiency of their tools hard to match.
They also contribute a lot to the open-source world and are sometimes valuable partners.
However, their private nature does not allow them to guarantee a space of total freedom and safety for all my personal data and conversations.
These can be analyzed and potentially shared with advertisers, insurance companies, AI engines, government services.
Technically, my data could also be confiscated, misappropriated and retained against my will, even if a serious professional would not do that.
It is therefore up to me to make the distinction.
:::
