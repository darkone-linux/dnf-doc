## My Sovereign and Secure Cloud

In summary:

- A **practical and secure digital space** for my data.
- **Useful services**: messaging, visio, documents, media, backups, games...
- **Pre-installed Linux systems** (optional) very easy to use.

:::tip[Ideal for children and teens]
A protected space suitable for youngers, for a controlled learning of computer tools and networks!
:::

---

{/* t:p 121d11183715dce64ba941be7309dd2ab422400f693d801e406c8a0a0fe085b9 */}
### Benefits

* 🔐 **Maximum security**: our data stays under our control and travels encrypted.
* 🚫 **No data leaks** to advertisers, AIs, insurance companies, tax authorities…
* 🔁 **Automated 3-2-1 backups**, encrypted, distributed, compressed.
* 🕐 **Top-notch apps**, stable, useful and simple, to save time.
* 🔑 **Single password** to remember (SSO + vault).
* 👶 **User-friendly** for all ages.

:::note[My digital legacy]
Photos, videos, stories, contacts, conversations, passwords… more and more memories and data tied to my legacy are now digital.
Using a safe, sovereign, and controlled place for this data, which makes up my identity, is essential. That is the very purpose of this cloud.
:::

:::tip[Should I quit Big Tech?]
No. These companies offer useful services. Thousands of engineers work there, making the simplicity and efficiency of their tools hard to match.
They also give a lot to the open-source world and are sometimes valuable partners.
However, their private nature means they cannot guarantee a completely free and safe space for all my personal data and conversations.
These can be analyzed and potentially shared with advertisers, insurance companies, AI engines, and government agencies.
Technically, my data could also be confiscated, misused, and retained against my will, even though a serious professional would not do that.
So it is up to me to draw the line.
:::
